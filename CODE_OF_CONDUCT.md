# Code of Conduct

This repository expects direct, respectful, and specific collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Critique the work, not the person.
- Keep issues and pull requests factual and actionable.
- Assume good faith while still holding a high bar for accuracy and quality.
- Respect boundaries around private, security-sensitive, or customer-sensitive information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of plugin behavior, status, or ownership.
- Publishing private credentials, tokens, or sensitive data.
- Spam, trolling, or low-effort noise that makes review harder.

## Maintainer enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that undermines a safe and productive collaboration environment.
